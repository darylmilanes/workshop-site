Explore all modules included in the Workplace Communication Training program:

Module 1: Foundations of Communication

Understanding Communication Styles
  • Diagnose your style via a workplace style mapping tool
  • “Style-switching” drills to adapt communication on the fly
  • Competitive scenario games: adapting to multiple styles in one conversation
Active Listening & Deep Questioning
  • Listening sprints with layered questioning (surface → deep insight)
  • “Echo then elevate” technique practice (paraphrase + add value)
  • Simulated miscommunication drills with instant correction
Verbal & Non-Verbal Synergy
  • Decode hidden meanings in facial expressions/body language
  • Video playback practice with peer critique
  • Contrast verbal tone with non-verbal signals in live role-play
Overcoming Barriers to Communication
  • “Noise filters” workshop: handle jargon, accents, distractions
  • Stress-testing messages under simulated workplace pressure
  • Multi-channel message relay (email + chat + call) to test clarity
Empathy in Conversations
  • “Empathy mapping” exercise: step into colleague/customer shoes
  • Reframe negative feedback with empathetic language drills
  • Competitive empathy challenge: win points for accurate perspective-taking
Psychological Safety Basics
  • Simulated unsafe-to-safe discussion conversions
  • Identify microaggressions & reframe into inclusive phrasing
  • Build “safe phrase” lists for your workplace
Clarity & Conciseness
  • 60-second clarity pitch (cutting down wordiness)
  • Rewrite long-winded emails into 3-line versions
  • “Clarity duel”: team competition for simplest explanation
Confidence vs Aggressiveness
  • Self-assessment role-plays to spot over-assertion
  • Controlled assertiveness scripts with progressive challenges
  • Group reflection on “impact vs intent” mismatches
Cross-Generational Communication
  • Translate Boomer/Millennial/Gen Z expectations in scenarios
  • Style-shifting exercises for mixed-age workplace teams
  • Generational stereotype challenge (debunk via real cases)
Mindful Communication
  • 3-minute mindful pauses before delivering feedback
  • Stress reduction drills before sensitive talks
  • Reframe reactive responses into mindful responses

Conciseness in Digital Workflows
